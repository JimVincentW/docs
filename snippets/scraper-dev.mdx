### Developing the Scraper Service
The Docker Image of the scraper service is equipped wih the most popular scraping and data manipulation libraries.
You cann use Selenium, BeautifulSoup, Playwright and of course libraries like Pandas.

<Info>
If you scraping scripts have any additional dependencies, remember to add them to the `requirements.txt` file.
</Info>
